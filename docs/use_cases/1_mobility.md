# Use Case 1: Mobility and Transportation Planning
**Purpose: To outline required functionality for the mobility specific use case**

## Types of Requests
*To be able to serve this use case, Geoff has to be able to answer these types of questions and assist with these types of analysis.*

**Infrastructure Inventory**
- Show all bike lanes added since 2020.
- List all protected bike lanes in the downtown core.
- How many kilometers of pedestrian pathways are in high-density neighborhoods?
- Which streets have new bus lanes this year?

**Proximity / Accessibility**
- Which schools are within 500 meters of a bike lane?
- Show all bus stops within 10 minutes walking distance of major hospitals.
- Highlight areas where no sidewalk exists within 300 meters of a school.

**Safety Analysis**
- Identify intersections with more than 5 accidents in the last 2 years.
- Show all streets with high crash frequency near pedestrian crossings.
- Which bike lanes are longer than 500 meters and intersect with high-speed roads?

**Gap / Coverage Analysis**
- Show neighborhoods without any protected bike lanes.
- Identify areas with poor transit coverage.
- Highlight streets that could benefit from new pedestrian infrastructure.

**Temporal / Change Over Time**
- How has total bike lane length changed in the last 5 years?
- Show the growth of new pedestrian infrastructure by neighborhood.
- Compare the number of transit stops added this year vs last year.
