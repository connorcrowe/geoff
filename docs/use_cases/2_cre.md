# Use Case 2: Commercial Real Estate Intelligence & Site Selection
**Purpose: To outline required functionality for the real estate specific use case**

## Types of Requests
*To be able to serve this use case, Geoff has to be able to answer these types of questions and assist with these types of analysis.*

**Location Suitability**
- Show parcels within 500 meters of subway stations.
- Which commercial parcels are near high-density residential areas?
- Identify sites close to grocery stores and major intersections.

**Zoning and Development**
- List parcels eligible for commercial development.
- Show parcels zoned for mixed-use in downtown neighborhoods.
- Which sites have restrictions on building height or lot coverage?

**Demographics / Market Access**
- Highlight parcels near neighborhoods with median income above $100k.
- Which sites are within walking distance of more than 2,000 residents?
- Show locations within 1 km of high foot-traffic areas.

**Comparative Scoring**
- Rank sites based on proximity to transit, population density, and competitors.
- Which locations have the highest combined accessibility to customers and suppliers?
- Compare potential parcels within a neighborhood by weighted score of amenities.

**Temporal / Development History**
- Show parcels that have recently changed zoning status.
- Identify properties that were developed in the last 2 years.
- Which sites have had new infrastructure (roads, transit) added nearby recently?
